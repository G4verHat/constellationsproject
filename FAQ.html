FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions


How do I get into Amauteur Astronomy?

Well, there is no right answer, but look at the sky and try to find out what constellations are there.
Get to know famous stars, try to find planets and such. just the naked eye can go a long way.


What is a constellation?

A constellation is a group of stars forming a recognizable pattern.
Usually named after a mythological creature or character, like Perseus, or Centaurus, for example.

I can't see a certain constellation/star(s)! What should I do?

There are two main reasons for that is happening.
Light Pollution: If you live in a highly urbanized city, you might suffer from that.
The lights of cars, lamps and other houses can make seeing less bright stars harder.


You can check which constellations are visible for you using a sky map, either physical or online using a website like stellarium.com
