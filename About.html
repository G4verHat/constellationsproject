About us

The constellations project is meant to be a tool from amauteur astronomers to one another.

Our purpose, aside being a fun little project to spend time on,
is to make all the beautiful constellations of our sky easy to find all in one place.

We sure love astronomy! and I hope you too!
We do not own any of the pictures on the website. All of them are taken from the internet.

Happy Skygazing!
